Homework

Homework

These assignments are typically larger and more intensive that tutorials or lectures are. They are typically done outside of class and test our knowledge of the content we've gone over in the past two or so weeks.

Tutorials

Tutorials

Though not usually as difficult as Homework assignments, Tutorials are often introduced during class time, and due a few days after that. They are assigned more often than homework assignments, though they also have a shorter period of time to do them, due to their lower difficulty.

Quizzes

Quizzes

Quizzes are meant to test our knowledge. They typically include a written portion (not included on this site) and a coding portion. Students strive to complete all given tasks to the best of thier ability before the class time is up, at which point the quiz closes.

Lectures

Lectures

Lectures are walk-throughs done during class time, where the instructor guides us. These are meant to reinforce the learning and readings that the class period centers on and to give the students hands-on experience.
